Q: How can I book a private plane through WildRydes?
A: Booking a private plane is easy with WildRydes. Simply download our app, browse available planes, select your preferred options, and follow the secure booking process.
Q: What types of private planes are available for rent?
A: We offer a diverse fleet, including small, medium, and large private planes. Whether you need a quick business trip or a luxurious travel experience, we have the perfect aircraft for you.
Q: How do I request a plane?
A: Simply download our app, then tap a button to begin. Your unicorn will arrive shortly.
Q: How does pricing work for private plane rentals?
A: Pricing varies based on the type of plane, duration, and additional services. Our app provides transparent pricing, allowing you to customize your experience and see the cost upfront.
Q: How does it work?
A: Our product is powered by a complex algorithm which efficiently matches idle planes with ryders based on factors such as proximity and shortest time-to-destination. The system is built on a serverless architecture, which makes running and scaling our backend services simple and cost-effective, allowing us to reliably serve the needs of Wild Rydes’ ever growing user base.
Q: What if I have a complaint about my plane?
A: Wild Rydes is a customer obsessed company. We value each customer and want to ensure a positive experience. Therefore, we’ve staffed our customer service team with serverless chatbots that are available 24/7 to assist you.
Q: How do I cancel my ride?
A: Tap the “Cancel Ryde” button in the Wild Rydes app.
Q: Can I use Wild Rydes internationally?
A: Yes, you can use Wild Rydes in most countries except for Antarctica, Cuba, Sudan, Iran, North Korea, Syria and any other country designated by the United States Treasury's Office of Foreign Assets Control.
Q: How do I pay for my ryde?
A: After creating a Wild Rydes account, fill in your payment method such as credit card, debit card, Bitcoin wallet, or Vespene gas repository. After you complete your Ryde, you will automatically be charged the fare.
Q: How many passengers can my plane take?
A: The number of passengers on a single ryde depends on the size of your plane. Most planes can take five passenger per ryde. You can also request a large size plane which can take up to 8 passengers. If you select Sleigh version, you can take up to 10 passengers.
Q: Are there membership programs or discounts available?
A: Yes, we offer membership programs and periodic promotions for our users. Check our app or website for the latest offers to make your private plane experience even more rewarding.
Q: How do I share my route information with someone else?
A: During your ryde, you can share your route and ETA with someone else using the Wild Rydes app. Simply tap the “Share Route” button and select a contact. Soon, they’ll be able to watch the status of your ryde.
Q: How do I rate my plane?
A: After your ryde completes, you have the option to rate your plane on the app. Our planes are customer obsessed and strive for 5 star ratings. Your feedback helps us improve our service!
Q: What if my plane doesn’t match the photo in the app?
A: The plane photo in your app should match the plane that arrives to pick you up. If they do not match, then Wild Rydes recommends that you do not board the plane. You should then immediately report the imposter plane to Wild Rydes.
Q: Can I use Concur with Wild Rydes?
A: While we do not allow requesting specific plane, you can choose the type and size of plane using the app.
Q: Why do you charge a service fee?
A: The service fee is a fixed charge added to every ryde. This helps us pay for our on-going maintenance and operating costs required to run the service four our aircraft.
Q: Can I bring pets on board?
A: Many of our private planes are pet-friendly. Be sure to mention your pet during the booking process, and our team will assist in making your furry friend's journey comfortable and enjoyable.
